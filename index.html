About the song

"Harvest Moon" is a song by Neil Young, released in 1992 as the title track of his album "Harvest Moon".

The song is a nostalgic reflection on love and the passage of time, often interpreted as a tribute to Young's then-wife, Pegi Young.

The lyrics evoke a sense of romantic longing and the desire to hold onto love as time goes by.

The song has become one of Neil Young's most beloved tracks and is often associated with themes of enduring love and the beauty of nature.

About the Artist

Neil Young is a Canadian-American singer-songwriter and musician known for his distinctive voice and introspective songwriting.

He has been active in the music industry since the 1960s, releasing over 40 studio albums. His work spans genres including folk, rock, and country music.

Young is also known foris environmental activism and his advocacy for various social causes.
